# Road Rush
Road Rush is a single player racing game built with pygame. Play our game and see how fast you can collect 20 coins!!!!

<img width="593" height="432" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/704a14f0-6e7b-4550-972f-b9d9e3e9b7b5" />

- Press the "Enter" to play
- Use your arrow keys or "WASD" to move your car along the track
- Avoid tires and oil spills
- Collect coins to win!
